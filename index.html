How It Works

From enquiry
to certification.

01
Initial Assessment
We assess your current licence level, logged hours, goals, and timeline. This shapes a programme precisely matched to where you are and where you need to be.
02
Programme Design
Our instructors design a structured syllabus — ground school, simulator sessions, and flying hours — with clear milestones and a realistic completion target.
03
Training Begins
You begin flying with our instructors or, for simulator courses, progress through the approved syllabus. Progress is monitored at every stage with detailed debrief reports.
04
Rating Achieved
Skill test, examiner sign-off, and licence endorsement. Our 100% first-attempt pass rate reflects the quality of preparation we put into every candidate.
